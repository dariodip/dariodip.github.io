I help B2B SaaS engineering teams improve delivery predictability without architecture hype.

What I focus on#

  • Reducing production instability and recurring critical bugs
  • Improving sprint predictability and execution discipline
  • Strengthening technical leadership and engineering ownership
  • Making release processes explicit, repeatable, and measurable

Engagement model#

Fractional Technical Lead + Senior Implementation Partner

  • Initial technical audit
  • 90-day engineering roadmap
  • Delivery process setup (review/release discipline, CI/CD practices)
  • Hands-on implementation on core components (Python/Elixir)
  • Mentoring for internal engineers and new technical leaders

Typical outcomes in 90 days#

  • Fewer critical defects reaching production
  • More stable sprint throughput and fewer delivery surprises
  • Clearer ownership and stronger technical decision-making

Who this is for#

Startup and scale-up teams that need better delivery reliability, stronger engineering structure, and pragmatic architecture decisions.

About

February 24, 2026 · 1 min · map[name:Dario Di Pasquale]

I am an Engineering Manager with a strong software engineering background in distributed systems, delivery execution, and pragmatic architecture.

Today I lead engineering in the Claims area at Prima, where I built and scaled teams around Data Management and Machine Learning initiatives. My work sits at the intersection of technical leadership, product delivery, and execution quality.

What I focus on#

  • Building engineering teams that deliver predictably
  • Turning architecture decisions into measurable delivery outcomes
  • Designing systems that are maintainable, scalable, and operationally stable
  • Coaching engineers and growing new technical leaders

Operating style#

I care about outcomes over hype.

That means:

  • clear ownership
  • strong engineering discipline
  • practical architecture choices
  • continuous alignment between product priorities and technical execution

Background#

Before my current role, I worked across DevOps, platform architecture, and backend engineering at Ericsson and Immobiliare.it, with hands-on work in high-availability and distributed environments.

Earlier in my career, I delivered freelance projects in backend, data aggregation, and blockchain-based authentication systems.

Education and certifications#

  • Professional Scrum Master I (Scrum.org)
  • M.Sc. in Networks and Software Technologies, University of Salerno (110 cum laude)
  • B.Sc. in Computer Science, University of Salerno (110 cum laude)

Outside work#

I enjoy trekking, climbing, mountaineering, tennis, and reading.
